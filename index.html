About

Who I Am

I'm a Mechanical Engineering graduate (2025) with a strong foundation in 3D modelling, parametric design, and automotive component development. My core expertise lies in plastic product design and BIW structures using CATIA V5 and Siemens NX, with practical exposure to injection moulding, sheet metal, surface handling, and A-to-B surface creation.

I spent a year as an internship instructor at CAD CAM Guru Solutions Pvt. Ltd., where I taught CATIA V5, Siemens NX, CREO, and ZW CAD to engineering students — a role that deepened my own technical mastery while sharpening my ability to communicate complex design concepts clearly.

Since October 2025, I've been part of the R&D team at Spark Minda (Minda Infac Pvt. Ltd.), contributing to New Product Development on automotive antenna systems — applying DFM principles, 3D modelling, and cross-functional collaboration to deliver production-ready designs. With 500+ completed design projects spanning assemblies, plastic parts, BIW, and reverse engineering, I bring both volume and variety to every design challenge.
